Selector
Greeter
The robot will greet the human with a salutation. Greeter is always the first micro in an interaction.
Ask
The robot will ask a question of your choosing to the human. Based on the human's response, the robot will trigger the Ready or Not Ready state.
Remark
The robot will make a remark of your choosing to the human.
Instruction
The robot will give an instruction of your choosing to the human.
Handoff
The robot will physically give something to the human.
Answer
The robot will answer any question the human asks. If the human proceeds to the Not Ready state, the robot did not successfully answer the question.
Wait
The robot will wait for the human for a specified duration of time.
Farewell
The robot will say goodbye to the human. Farewell is always the last micro in an interaction.
